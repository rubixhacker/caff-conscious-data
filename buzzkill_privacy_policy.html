Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 25, 2025

1. Introduction

At Buzzkill, our mission is to help you reclaim your focus, not your data. We believe your notifications are private conversations, not commodities. This Privacy Policy explains how Buzzkill collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

The short version: Buzzkill is designed to work 100% on your device. We do not send your notification content, contacts, or app usage history to any server. The only data that ever leaves your device is related to verifying your Premium subscription status via our payment provider.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

To be explicitly clear, Buzzkill does not transmit, store, or share the following data with us or any third party:

  • Notification Content: The text, titles, and images within your notifications are processed locally on your device by our Machine Learning engine and never leave your phone.
  • App Usage History: Which apps you use and when you use them stays on your device.
  • Personal Identifiers: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

3. Information We Collect

Buzzkill strictly limits data collection to what is necessary for the app's subscription functionality.

3.1. Purchase and Subscription Data

We use a trusted third-party service, RevenueCat, to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions (e.g., Buzzkill Pro). When you purchase a subscription:

  • What is processed: An anonymous App User ID, your purchase history (e.g., "Monthly Subscription"), and the expiration date of your subscription.
  • Why it is processed: To validate your purchase status and unlock Pro features across your devices or after a reinstall.
  • Privacy: This data is linked to your Google Play Store account ID, not your personal identity within our app. We do not have access to your credit card information or billing address; that is handled exclusively by Google Play.

4. Android Permissions

Buzzkill requires specific permissions to function. Here is how we use them:

  • Notification Access (NotificationListenerService):
    • Purpose: Required to read incoming notifications so the on-device AI can classify them as "Signal" or "Noise" and dismiss spam on your behalf.
    • Privacy Guarantee: This access is used strictly for local processing. No data read by this service is transmitted to the cloud.
  • Network Access:
    • Purpose: Used only to communicate with RevenueCat to verify your subscription status. It is not used to upload user data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use only one third-party service to support our app's core business model:

  • RevenueCat:
    • We use RevenueCat to power our in-app subscriptions.
    • For more information on how RevenueCat handles data, please visit the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect your personal data or notification content, there is no central database of user information to hack. Your personal data stays secured on your physical device, protected by Android's sandboxing and encryption features.
